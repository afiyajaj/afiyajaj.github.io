Summary
Challenge:
People all over canada experience food insecurity, many of which don't have easy access to food banks and other similar facilities. meanwhile, food wastage is at an all time high. Clients would like to see app that will allow people to get access to food where it is available near them. It needs to be easily accessible for those who do and those who do not have a mobile device. It also needs to be easily accessible for those who do not have great reading or great mobility. Needless needs to have a map to allow those who cannot go far, have access to food that is nearest them.

Core principles
The needless app will need to have health and safety at its forefront. It must Encompass Detailed Profiles, Vetting process during Signup, Designated Meetup Areas, address Privacy Concerns, Rating System and the Ability  to Report Users. it will also need to address the Longevity of the app. this is accomplished by User Participation, Leaderboard Reward System, Sponsors and a Badge System.
